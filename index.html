About Me
I grew up on the east coast of Australia, in the leafy green suburbs of sunny Brisbane. I did my undergraduate studies at the University of Queensland, completing degrees in Science and Arts, majoring in physics and French. I graduated in 2020, and was the Faculty of Science Valedictorian.
In 2021 I moved to Amsterdam to start my PhD under the supervision of Dr. Arghavan Safavi-Naini, in the Hyqs group. From 2026 I will be a Rubicon postdoctoral fellow, hosted at the University of Maryland in Alexey Gorshkov's group.
